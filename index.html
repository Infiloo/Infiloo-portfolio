About

I'm Leo, also known as Infiloo — a developer focused on practical, privacy-minded software across web, mobile, and embedded platforms.

My work spans custom chat applications, cryptographic messaging tools, automation bots, and media integrations. I'm most at home on a Raspberry Pi or an ESP32, wiring up self-hosted services and small hardware experiments that make everyday computing feel personal again.

Primary Hardware Raspberry Pi 500 · ESP32 · Microcontrollers
